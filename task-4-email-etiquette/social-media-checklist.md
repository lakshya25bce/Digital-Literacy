### Social Media Etiquette Checklist

As a Digital Ambassador, maintaining a professional online presence is essential. Below is a checklist of responsible social media practices for college students.

### Do's 
* **Update your LinkedIn:** Keep your profile current with your latest projects and Python skills.
* **Professional Language:** Use respectful and clear communication in technical forums like Stack Overflow or GitHub.
* **Share Milestones:** Post about academic achievements, such as certifications, hackathons, or club events (like NeuroXAI).
* **Verify Privacy:** Regularly check and update the privacy settings on your personal accounts (Instagram, Facebook).
* **Fact-Check:** Verify the accuracy of technical news or university announcements before sharing them.

### Don'ts 
* **Leaking Info:** Don't post confidential details, question papers, or internal photos from university labs.
* **Online Arguments:** Don't engage in public wars or use unprofessional slang/abusive language in comments.
* **Spreading Misinformation:** Don't share unverified fake news or controversial content that could harm your reputation.
* **Over-tagging:** Don't tag professors or university officials in personal, informal, or unrelated photos.
* **Public Complaining:** Don't use professional platforms to post public grievances about peers, faculty, or the institution.
